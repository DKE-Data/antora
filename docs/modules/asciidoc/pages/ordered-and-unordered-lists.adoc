== Ordered list syntax

.Ordered list with nested levels
[source,asciidoc]
----
.Optional Title
. Step 1
. Step 2
.. Details
... Mini-details
.... Micro-details
..... We're really down in the weeds now.
.. More details
. Step 3
----

.Result
--
.Optional Title
. Step 1
. Step 2
.. Details
... Mini-details
.... Micro-details
..... We're really down in the weeds now.
.. More details
. Step 3
--

The maximum nesting level is five (`pass:[.....]`).

== Unordered list syntax

.Unordered list with nested levels
[source,asciidoc]
----
.Optional Title
* Item A
* Item B
** Item B1
*** Details
**** More details
***** Details about the details
** Item B2
* Item C
----

.Result
--
.Optional Title
* Item A
* Item B
** Item B1
*** Details
**** More details
***** Details about the details
** Item B2
* Item C
--

The maximum nesting level is five (`pass:[*****]`).
